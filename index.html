Mila Nery is a Brazilian Graphic Designer and 3D Artist now based in Brooklyn. She graduated from the Maryland Institute College of Art in the Spring of 2016. Since then, she's moved to Brooklyn, started freelancing around, and adopted a pup named Tiva. In her spare time she likes to play video games, sew, and go for long walks.
